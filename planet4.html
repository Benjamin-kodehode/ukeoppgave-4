Kamino

Kamino is an ocean-covered planet located in the Outer Rim, known for its advanced cloning facilities. The planet's ecosystems primarily consist of vast oceans and stormy weather, with very little landmass available. Its area is estimated to be around 30,000 square kilometers, with a circumference of approximately 10,000 kilometers. The population of Kamino is small, around 1 million, mainly consisting of Kaminoans who are skilled in cloning technology. Kamino is most famous for housing the cloning facilities that produced the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars.
