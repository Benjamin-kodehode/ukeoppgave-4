Tatooine

Tatooine is a desert planet located in the Outer Rim Territories, recognized for its twin suns and arid climate. The planet's ecosystems are characterized by vast desert landscapes with limited vegetation, primarily consisting of moisture farming crops and hardy scrub vegetation. Wildlife on Tatooine includes unique creatures like Banthas and the nomadic Tusken Raiders. Covering approximately 104,000 square kilometers, Tatooine has a circumference of about 22,000 kilometers and a population of around 2 million, primarily made up of humans, Jawas, and various alien species. The planet is famous for its bustling spaceports, such as Mos Eisley, and serves as the home world of key characters like Anakin and Luke Skywalker.
