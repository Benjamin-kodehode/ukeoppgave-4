Mustafar

Mustafar is a volcanic planet in the Outer Rim, known for its fiery landscape and harsh climate. The planet's ecosystems are largely dominated by volcanic activity, featuring lava rivers, volcanic vents, and rugged terrains that support only the hardiest life forms. Its area is estimated to be around 20,000 square kilometers, with a circumference of approximately 8,000 kilometers. The population on Mustafar is sparse, primarily consisting of miners and individuals who exploit its valuable resources. It is infamous for being the location of the duel between Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi, as well as for its role in the creation of the Galactic Empire.
