Coruscant

Coruscant is a vibrant, city-covered planet located in the Core Worlds of the Star Wars galaxy. Its entire surface is urbanized, dominated by towering skyscrapers and extensive infrastructure, making it one of the most densely populated places in the galaxy. Due to its urban environment, natural ecosystems are virtually nonexistent; however, artificial parks and biodomes are created to mimic natural habitats. Coruscant's circumference is estimated to be around 24,000 kilometers, and it is home to trillions of inhabitants from various species, serving as the political hub of the galaxy where the Galactic Senate and Jedi Temple are located.
